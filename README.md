# Trader Performance vs Market Sentiment Analysis

## Methodology
We analyzed trader performance and behavior under Fear and Greed market sentiment.
The datasets were cleaned, merged by date, and key metrics such as PnL, trade frequency, position size, and long/short ratio were analyzed.

## Insights
1. Trader profitability is higher during Greed markets.
2. Traders trade more frequently during Fear markets.
3. Position sizes are larger during Greed periods.
4. Short positions are more common during Fear markets.

## Strategy Recommendations
1. During Fear markets, traders should reduce position sizes and avoid overtrading.
2. During Greed markets, traders should increase position sizes and follow trend-based strategies.
